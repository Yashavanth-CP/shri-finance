Understanding Your Fund Options

📈Investment Advantages

  • Attractive return potential — direct participation in capital market performance over the long term
  • Excellent selection — choose up to 20 funds from approximately 70 options across leading global providers
  • Specialist retirement funds — three managed fund portfolios specifically optimised for pension accumulation
  • Cost-efficient ETFs — low-expense-ratio index trackers that maximise your net returns
  • Sustainable funds available — ESG-screened portfolios for the values-conscious investor

🔄Full Fund Flexibility

  • Flexible fund switches — reallocate holdings or redirect future contributions, up to 12× per year free
  • Annual rebalancing — automatically readjust to your chosen risk profile each year
  • Automated lifecycle management — in the final five years, gradually shift into secured assets until 100% protected

🔧Life Flexibility Features

  • Adjust or pause contributions — reduce, increase or temporarily suspend premiums anytime
  • Choose your income type — lifetime pension, single lump sum, or a flexible combination of both
  • Partial withdrawals — access funds both before and after retirement begins
  • Move your retirement date — bring it forward or push it back as life circumstances change
  • Optional disability cover — add or remove occupational disability protection at any stage
  • Death benefit — insured sum without health questions (3-year wait; waived for accidental death)

⚖Tax Efficiency

  • Tax-deferred growth — contributions grow within the plan with no annual capital gains tax
  • Pension income tax advantage — structured to minimise your effective tax rate in retirement
  • Lump-sum option — strategic partial capital withdrawal to reduce lifetime tax burden
